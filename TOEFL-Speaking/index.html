本人口语23的归档

话题

Personality

Outgoing

  • Being “outgoing” involves learning how to present yourself to others, striking up conversations, and being more confident in yourself.

Independent

  • I don’t need to always ask for my parents for money for what I like

Selfish

  • I hate/dislike/detest selfish people, since they always care for their own sake and calculate gain and loss, even when making friends.
  • They would never feel grateful when they receive help from others.

Interest

  • provide students more freedom to choose what they are truly interested in
  • recognize the importance of personalized education

Stress

  • xxx keeps us company and reduces my stress and loneliness.
  • the massive load of assignments suffocates students.
  • with lower pressure, learn more healthily and sustainably

Eating

  • one of the most satisfying to do on planet earth
  • gain insights on into the local culinary culture & life style of people
  • being food fanatics, sample the food
  • the quintessential jewel of Chinese traditional dumpling
  • gain insights into the lifestyle of Chinese people
  • different combinations of vegetables, meats, and seafood
  • provide all necessary nutrition a student is going to need for a day

Cooperation

  • share ideas, materials, and resources with other group members
  • various ideas begin to forge into a master plan and design
  • rush together towards a deadline
  • overcome difficulties
  • a consensus may be hard to reach, it provides chance to negotiate and coordinate, which would be very useful for one’s future career.

Study

  • participating more in class discussion, asking and answering questions, finish assignment at high quality, and performing better in every quiz, mid-term and final.
  • stop being anonymous in the class and put himself on the professor’s radar.
  • get the professor’s attention and let the professor know that he’s working this hard.

Stay alone

  • spend time alone in civilized recreational activities, such as reading a book, writing a prose, or just think about what happens to me and around me.
  • when people come and go, it creates a lot of noise, the place gets messy and crowded.
  • add more exhaustion to my already busy life.
  • nobody rushes me, nobody distracts me and interferes me with whatever I do
  • concentrate on my personal stuffs.

Shopping online

  • internet shopping is better than shopping in an actual shop
  • frees customers of travel problem
  • crowded subway train and the lack of enough parking spaces often make an otherwise pleasant experience painful.
  • online shopping doesn’t cause much anxiety and fatigue
  • buying things are no longer available in a store
  • a better economic policy

Friendship

  • senior roommate for freshman
  • offer experience and share resources
  • give constructive suggestion and first-hand experience to the newcomer, in light of the best cafeteria, the least crowded gym, the most helpful prof.
  • open new doors for new student to campus by introducing alumni circles, interviews and internship opportunities
  • this compatibility provides good foundation for friendship to emerge
  • people who go to the same session may have the same values and goals in life, and their interests, hobbies and tastes might also be quiet similar.
  • we can catch a movie, hang out in a coffee house, or shoot some hoop
  • really get to know each other

Paper

  • research paper works most to the students’ benefits because it’s an active learning experience.
  • exhaust resources both from the library and from the internet.
  • provides healthy pressure for sustained learning
  • the accuracy and validity of such information is often insured.

High-tech devices

  • disturb and disrupt others’ experiences in public places, such as restaurants, libraries and cinemas.
  • can often interfere with other machines and equipments even without awareness.
  • electronic magnetic interference your cellphone sends out will put your or others people’s life in jeopardy.

Education

  • my generation and my younger generation are probably by far the best educated youth
  • there is an increasing majority of the younger population who love books right now

Travel

  • be free from pressure and anxiety
  • if I pay th e money for travel agency, the tour guide will handle everything, for my air tickets, itinerary, food and accommodation, to provide me heads-up to cultural differences and possible danger.
  • lack of flexibility and freedom
  • incompatible values and tastes
  • unique, one-of-a-kind experience: X duplicated & cloned
  • be impulsive, intuitive, adventurous

Parent’s decision

  • be involved in the college decision of
  • choose the right major field with their extensive work experience and assist the child to decide the right campus with their life wisdom
  • understand their child better than does the child
  • parents’ advice in college application and decision is paramount for the child’s well-being.
  • their roles should be facilitate, not to dominate

Others

Pets

  • xxx offers companionship;
  • provide the opportunity to love and be loved.

Arts

  • masterpiece of painting

Preference

  1. prefer A to B
  2. like A better than B
  3. what I like most
  4. I’d rather A than B

Comparasion

  1. the difference between … is that … is more while … is more…
  2. one big way that … differ from … is
  3. … tend to be more … while …

Dichotomy

  1. There are two sides of this issue.
  2. The issue is complex. On the one hand …
  3. There are many adavantages and disadvantages to …
    benefits/problems;
    strengths/weaknesses;
    pros/cons;
  4. The positive side of … is, but the darker side of it is …

Addition

  1. in addition, I think we should consider
  2. besides
  3. what’s more

Change

  1. There have been some big changes
  2. Some big changes have taken place

Agreement

  1. That’s absolutely true
  2. I totally agree with you on this.
  3. I’m with you on this

Disagreement

  1. I’m against your opinion
  2. I disagree with … entirely
  3. I wouldn’t accept … for a minute
  4. I’m inclined to disagree with that

Possibility

  1. I doubt if …
  2. What he told us was unbelievable
    it’s quiet possible …

Expressions

  1. … is a small cost to pay
  2. live through different things
  3. lower one’s standard
  4. groan about
  5. plan ahead
